The Parties / Τα Μέρη

(1)
The Disclosing Party / Το Αποκαλύπτον Μέρος: The individual researcher and founder operating under the business name HealthLedgerAI, conducting doctoral research on billing compliance and cooperation agreements in private healthcare in Cyprus and Greece (hereinafter "HealthLedgerAI" or "the Disclosing Party"); and
(2)
The Receiving Party / Το Λαμβάνον Μέρος: EMBRYOLAB FERTILITY CLINIC, with registered address at Leoforos Ethnikis Antistaseos 173–175, Kalamaria, 55134, Thessaloniki, Greece, ISO 9001:2015 and EN 15224:2017 certified (hereinafter "Embryolab" or "the Receiving Party").

This Mutual Non-Disclosure and Confidentiality Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date last signed below. HealthLedgerAI intends to disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to Embryolab for the sole purpose of evaluating a potential research collaboration or business relationship (the "Purpose"). Both parties agree to protect such information under the following terms.

1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by HealthLedgerAI to Embryolab, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by demonstration, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential. Without limitation, Confidential Information specifically includes:

  • The concept, design, architecture, and methodology of the HealthLedgerAI product, platform, and AI system
  • All doctoral research findings, data, analyses, fieldwork notes, and conclusions related to billing compliance in private healthcare in Cyprus and Greece
  • Business strategy, market analysis, go-to-market plans, pricing models, and financial projections
  • Technical documentation, software architecture, AI training methodology, and product roadmaps
  • The identity of current or prospective clients, partners, and investors
  • All information — in Greek or English — disclosed in any presentation, demonstration, or meeting between the parties
  • The existence and terms of this Agreement itself

2. Obligations of the Receiving Party

Embryolab agrees to:

  • Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and protect it with at least the same degree of care used for their own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care
  • Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party, including employees, contractors, or advisors, except those with a strict need to know for the Purpose, and only after such persons have agreed in writing to obligations no less restrictive than those in this Agreement
  • Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose of evaluating a potential research collaboration or business relationship with HealthLedgerAI, and for no other purpose
  • Promptly notify HealthLedgerAI in writing upon discovery of any unauthorised use or disclosure of Confidential Information
  • Not copy, reproduce, or summarise Confidential Information except as strictly necessary for the Purpose

3. Non-Use and Non-Development

Embryolab agrees that it will not, directly or indirectly, use any Confidential Information to:

  • Develop, design, or procure any product, software, service, or methodology that is substantially similar to or derived from the HealthLedgerAI product or concept
  • Implement, adapt, or apply any methodology, framework, or system described in the Confidential Information without the express written consent of HealthLedgerAI
  • Brief, instruct, or engage any third party — including technology vendors, AI companies, consultants, or developers — to build or replicate any aspect of the HealthLedgerAI concept

4. Non-Circumvention

Embryolab agrees not to circumvent HealthLedgerAI by contacting, engaging, or entering into any agreement with any person, company, institution, or research body identified by HealthLedgerAI during the course of disclosures, where such contact relates directly or indirectly to the subject matter of the Confidential Information, without the prior written consent of HealthLedgerAI. This clause applies for a period of three (3) years from the date of last disclosure.

5. Exclusions

The obligations of confidentiality shall not apply to information that Embryolab can demonstrate by written evidence:

  • Was already in the public domain at the time of disclosure, or subsequently enters the public domain through no fault of Embryolab
  • Was already rightfully known to Embryolab without restriction prior to disclosure
  • Was independently developed by Embryolab without use of or reference to the Confidential Information
  • Is required to be disclosed by applicable Greek law, EU regulation, or court or regulatory order, provided that HealthLedgerAI is given prompt written notice and the opportunity to seek a protective order

6. Intellectual Property & Research Ownership

Nothing in this Agreement grants Embryolab any licence, right, title, or interest in or to any Confidential Information, or in any intellectual property of HealthLedgerAI. All Confidential Information — including the doctoral research, AI methodology, product concept, and any intellectual property embedded therein — remains the exclusive property of HealthLedgerAI. No disclosure under this Agreement shall be construed as granting any rights under any patent, copyright, trade secret, or other intellectual property right.

7. Return or Destruction of Materials

Upon written request from HealthLedgerAI, or upon termination of discussions between the parties for any reason, Embryolab shall promptly return or certify the destruction of all Confidential Information in their possession, including all copies, notes, presentations, summaries, and derivative works, in any format or medium, and shall confirm such return or destruction in writing within ten (10) business days.

8. Term

This Agreement shall remain in force for a period of five (5) years from the date of last signature. The obligations of confidentiality under Clauses 2, 3, and 4 shall survive the expiration or termination of this Agreement with respect to Confidential Information received during its term. The non-circumvention obligations under Clause 4 shall survive for three (3) years from the date of last disclosure.

9. Remedies

Embryolab acknowledges that any breach or threatened breach of this Agreement would cause HealthLedgerAI irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy. HealthLedgerAI shall be entitled, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity under Greek law or EU law, to seek injunctive or other equitable relief from any competent court, without the requirement to post any bond, prove actual damages, or exhaust other remedies first.

10. GDPR Compliance

Both parties agree to handle any personal data exchanged in connection with this Agreement in compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Greek implementing legislation. No personal data of clinic patients, staff, or third parties shall be included in any Confidential Information exchanged under this Agreement without the prior written agreement of both parties and appropriate legal basis.

11. No Obligation to Proceed

Nothing in this Agreement obligates either party to proceed with any business relationship, transaction, or further discussions. Either party may terminate discussions at any time without liability, subject to the ongoing confidentiality obligations set out herein.

12. Entire Agreement & Amendments

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior discussions, understandings, and agreements relating to confidentiality. This Agreement may not be amended except by a written instrument signed by duly authorised representatives of both parties.

13. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Hellenic Republic (Greece). Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts of Thessaloniki, Greece. The parties agree that the English language version of this Agreement shall be controlling in the event of any conflict with any translation.
