The Problem

Private clinics lose money on billing errors they cannot see

Σφάλματα χρέωσης που περνούν απαρατήρητα

🔴 Duplicate Invoices

The same procedure billed twice to the same insurer — often months apart. Insurers detect these and claw back payments with penalties.

🔴 Upcoding

A procedure billed above its EOPYY or insurer maximum rate. A single audit trigger that puts your entire insurer contract at risk.

🔴 Phantom Billing

An invoice raised for a date when the patient has no recorded appointment. In a routine audit, this looks like fraud.

🟡 Unbundling

Procedure components billed individually instead of as a bundle — inflating the total without clinical justification.

Relevance to Embryolab

Why fertility clinics are a natural fit

Γιατί οι κλινικές γονιμότητας έχουν ιδιαίτερα σύνθετη χρέωση

⚕

EOPYY + Private Insurers Simultaneously

IVF is covered by EOPYY up to 3 cycles for women under 50. Clinics bill two systems with different rules for the same patient — mixed billing is exactly where errors concentrate.

€

High Procedure Values

A single IVF cycle costs €3,000–€6,000. One upcoding or duplicate billing error at this scale represents significant financial exposure per incident.

📋

Multi-Step Billing Sequences

Each cycle generates 8–12 separate billing events. Date mismatches and duplicates are common at this volume — and they accumulate silently.

✓

ISO-Certified Environment

Embryolab holds ISO 9001:2015 and EN 15224 certification. A billing compliance tool fits naturally into an already process-driven, audit-ready environment.
